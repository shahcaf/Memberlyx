How do I add the bot?

Click the Invite Bot button on this page. You'll be redirected to Discord's authorization page. Select your server, review the permissions, and confirm. Memebrlxy will be ready to use immediately after joining.

Is Memebrlxy free?

Yes! Memebrlxy is free to use. Invite the bot to your server and start growing your community at no cost. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but core functionality will always remain accessible.

What permissions are required?

Memebrlxy requires Administrator permissions to function properly. This ensures the bot can manage members, configure settings, and perform server management tasks effectively. You can adjust permissions after setup if needed.

How do I get support?

Join our Support Server on Discord. Our team and community are there to help with setup, troubleshooting, and feature requests.
